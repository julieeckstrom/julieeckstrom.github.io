Julie is a product designer living in Pittsburgh, PA.

Uber

Design for self-driving cars. Projects include designing internal application suites enabling the development of autonomous vehicles, and designing visualizations for Uber riders in a self-driving car.

Bossa Nova Robotics

Bossa Nova creates robots that automate inventory processes in big-box grocery stores. Work here included researching and designing multi-modal human-robot interaction experiences, in addition to leading and designing internal fleet management tools.

DSG

Design for mobile e-commerce. Projects include leading the redesign of the Dick's Sporting Goods mobile experience, in-store kiosk interactions, and leading a ground-up design of a DSG collaborator, CALIA.

Branding Brand

Branding Brand is a full service agency for mobile ecommerce products. Work here included the interaction design and user testing for mobile sites for Costco, Sephora, and American Eagle, tablet sites for Lumber Liquidators, and mobile apps for Barnes & Noble and Sephora, in addition to many others.
